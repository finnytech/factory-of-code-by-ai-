# Chronosphere Task Citadel

Chronosphere Task Citadel is a unique blend of a productivity app and a city-builder game.
You are the architect of a digital citadel. By completing your real-world tasks, you gather resources (Chronotons) to expand and fortify your citadel against the relentless tide of entropy.

Features:
- Add and manage tasks.
- Earn resources by completing tasks.
- Spend resources to upgrade your citadel (visualized on a canvas).
- Real-time save functionality using localStorage.

## Running

Just open `index.html` in your favorite modern browser, or use a live server.
